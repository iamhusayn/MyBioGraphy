My Biography


Husayn was born into the linage of Abdulrazaq Alawiye. Being the second from a family of three. He hails from the creeks of lagos Island - A community filled with dynamic and resilient minds.

Husayn had his primary education from Our-Lady of Apostles, yaba, Lagos and completd his secondary education inFederal Science and technical College, Ilesa, Osun State.


Family Background

He was born into the family of Abdul-razak Alawiye (A down-to-earth secondary school teacher) and Riskiat Jimoh (A self-made seamstress) and teacher. He is the second of a twin (Kehinde) popularly referred to as "Ibeji" in yoruba land. He is followed by two younger siblings.


Education

Haven been born on the island, Husayn began his nursury education at Lolly-Day Nursery and Primary school in the late 90's and later moved to MaryLand Convent Private School, Mande for few months due to family relocation.

He gradauted to begin his secondary education as a boarding student at the Comprehensive High School, Aiyetoro, Yewa-North, Ogun State. He was later moved to the Federal Science and Technical College, Ilesa, Osun State.

In 2011, He was admitted to Kwara State University, Malete, where he graduated with a BSc. in Geology an Mineral Science.


Work Life

Husayn started his career as a customer service representative in a financial institution in Nigeria for 2 years, where he later moved into the fintech space as a social media specialist for about 6 months. He was promoted to a senior role and subsequently handled training for new hires.
